Last Translation Benchmark

This platform gathers inputs (text, video, audio, images, documents) that are challenging for modern machine translation systems.

Contributors submit these inputs alongside machine translation outputs and a verification rule. With 10 approved submissions, contributors are eligible for inclusion in the upcoming research publication.
